Dynamic and static websites

There are categorically two types of website,based on achitecture:

  1. Dynamic website
  2. Static website

      3. A website is a digital medium to spread information It was designed for Global reach

      What are Dynamic Websites?

      Dynamic websites are websites that delivers its content from the server, they are structured and scripted differently. They have a functioning and managed backend achitecture thereby requiring more expertise to set-up. The backend is where the information it receives from users are stored.

      What are Static Websites?

      static websites just like its name are static:in the sense that they do not receieve thier contents from server..they are pre-compiled. Static websites are the oldest known form of website,they do not require server-side codes there by fewer expertise are employed to produce results
